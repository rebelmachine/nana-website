Nana Kwaku Bonsam is an African Traditional Priest based in Ghana. In the last 20 years, he has been helping thousands of people around the world to achieve their goals in life and fortify their spiritual selves.

His commitment is to prove that, despite the impact of Chrisitanity and colonialism, there is Real Power in Africa.

Nana travels regularly and lived in New York, Amsterdam, Berlin, Italy. The efficacy of his Powers made him the most known and recognized traditionalist of Africa. He is regularly invited to attend international TV shows, radio programs and live events. Nana Kwaku Bonsam is also featured in two profile articles on The New York Times.
